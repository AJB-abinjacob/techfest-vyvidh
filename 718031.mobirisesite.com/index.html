About Vyvidh Fest

Welcome to the wildest ride of your life! Vyvidh is not just a fest; it’s a full-blown carnival of creativity and technology!

Since its inception, Vyvidh has transformed into a colossal gathering for tech enthusiasts and creative geniuses from all corners of the state. It’s where ideas collide, and innovation takes flight!

Mark your calendars for March 31st and April 1st, 2025, because this year’s edition promises to be bigger, bolder, and downright unforgettable! Get ready for a whirlwind of tech, art, music, and pure unadulterated fun!
